=== METLIFE STADIUM — FIFA WORLD CUP 2026 OFFICIAL RULES & POLICIES ===
Stadium Full Name: MetLife Stadium
Location: One MetLife Stadium Drive, East Rutherford, New Jersey 07073, USA
Capacity: 82,500 seats
Event: FIFA World Cup 2026

=== GATE INFORMATION & QUEUE SPEEDS ===
MetLife Stadium has 4 main entry points:
- Gate A (North Concourse - fully accessible step-free entrance, recommended for wheelchair users and families with strollers). Wait time: ~2 min.
- Gate B (East Concourse - features escalator structures, moderate traffic). Wait time: ~8 min.
- Gate C (South Concourse - ticket booth area, heavy congestion alert, please redirect fans to other gates). Wait time: ~22 min.
- Gate D (West Concourse - stairs access only, fast entry). Wait time: ~3 min.
Gates open 3 hours before kickoff. VIP/Hospitality entrances are via Gate A and Gate D only.

=== DIRECTIONS TO THE STADIUM ===
By Public Transit: Take the Meadowlands Rail Line B from Secaucus Junction or Hoboken Terminal directly to the MetLife Stadium Light Rail Terminal (far left of venue). Trains run every 10 minutes on match days starting 4 hours before kickoff.
By Bus: NJ Transit Bus Route 160 runs from Port Authority Bus Terminal (Manhattan) to MetLife Stadium on match days.
By Car from Manhattan: Take Lincoln Tunnel → NJ Route 3 West → Meadowlands Sports Complex exit. Follow signs to MetLife Stadium. Drive time: ~30 min without traffic.
By Car from Newark Airport (EWR): Take NJ Turnpike North → Exit 16W → Route 3 East → Meadowlands exit. Drive time: ~20 min.
By Rideshare (Uber/Lyft): Designated pickup/drop-off zone is in Lot K (northeast corner). Follow the blue rideshare signs.

=== PARKING ===
General Parking: Lots A, B, D, E, G, J, K, L, P — $40 per vehicle (pre-purchase recommended via Ticketmaster).
Accessible/ADA Parking: Lots E and G (closest to Gate A accessible entrance). Free for valid ADA permit holders.
EV Charging Stations: Located in Lot C (12 Tesla Superchargers, 8 universal Level 2 chargers). First-come, first-served.
Tailgating: Permitted in Lots E, G, J, K, L. Charcoal and propane grills allowed. No open flames after kickoff.

=== FOOD & BEVERAGE ===
Food Courts: Located on Concourse Level 1 (Sections 101-140) and Concourse Level 2 (Sections 201-240).
Main Food Court Area: Between Sections 115 and 120 on Level 1 (largest selection).
Cuisine Options: American (burgers, hot dogs, pizza), Mexican (tacos, burritos), Asian (noodles, sushi), Halal, Kosher, Vegan/Vegetarian options available.
Premium Dining: MetLife Club Restaurant (accessible from Sections 207-211 with premium ticket holders only).
Water Refill Stations: Free filtered water refill stations located near Sections 105, 118, 130, 209, and 225.
Alcohol: Beer and wine available at all concession stands. Hard liquor available at premium bars only. Sales stop at the 75th minute of the match. Valid ID required (21+).

=== SECURITY AND PROHIBITED ITEMS ===
All fans must pass through metal detectors and bag screening at entry gates.
Prohibited items inside the arena include:
- Bags larger than 12x6x12 inches (clear plastic/vinyl bags only are permitted).
- External food and beverages (one sealed water bottle under 20oz is allowed).
- Glass bottles and metal containers.
- Professional cameras and recording gear (lens longer than 6 inches).
- Weapons of any kind, pyrotechnics, flares, and smoke devices.
- Drones and unmanned aerial vehicles.
- Umbrellas (ponchos are allowed).
- Noisemakers (air horns, vuvuzelas, whistles).
- Large flags or banners (over 2x3 feet) unless pre-approved by FIFA.
For suspicious behavior, volunteers must maintain visual contact from a safe distance and notify supervisor on radio channel 2. Do not attempt direct containment.

=== RESTROOMS ===
Restrooms are located at every section entrance on both Concourse Level 1 and Level 2.
Family Restrooms: Near Sections 109, 125, 205, and 220.
Accessible Restrooms: Available at every restroom location, clearly marked with ADA signage.

=== LOST & FOUND ===
Lost & Found office is located at Guest Services near Section 116, Concourse Level 1.
Items can also be reported via the MetLife Stadium mobile app or by calling +1 (201) 559-1515.
Items are held for 30 days after the event.

=== FIRST AID & MEDICAL ===
First Aid stations are located near Section 109 (Level 1) and Section 215 (Level 2).
AED (Automated External Defibrillator) units are located at every gate entrance and every other section.
Nursing/Mother's Room: Located near Section 125 (Level 1).

=== WIFI & CONNECTIVITY ===
Free WiFi: Network name "MetLife_FIFA2026" — no password required, 50 Mbps per device.
Charging Stations: USB charging kiosks located near Sections 105, 118, 130, 209, and 225.

=== MERCHANDISE & FAN SHOPS ===
Official FIFA Fan Shops: Located at Gate A Plaza (outdoor), Section 110 (Level 1), and Section 210 (Level 2).
Hours: Open 4 hours before kickoff, close 1 hour after final whistle.
Payment: Cashless only (credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay). No cash accepted.

=== RE-ENTRY POLICY ===
Re-entry is NOT permitted. Once you exit the stadium, you cannot re-enter with the same ticket.
Exception: Medical emergencies — fans escorted out by medical staff may re-enter at Gate A with verified wristband.

=== WEATHER POLICY ===
MetLife Stadium is an open-air venue. Matches proceed in rain, cold, or heat.
In case of lightning: Play is suspended, fans must remain seated or move to covered concourse areas. Do NOT seek shelter under trees in parking lots.
Free poncho distribution points: Gate A and Gate D entrances during rain events.

=== SMOKING POLICY ===
MetLife Stadium is a 100% smoke-free venue. No smoking, vaping, or e-cigarettes anywhere inside the stadium or on concourses.
Designated Smoking Areas: Outside Gate D (re-entry NOT permitted if you exit to smoke).
